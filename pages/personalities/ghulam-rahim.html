Arbab Ghulam Rahim: The Active Chief Minister of Sindh

Dr. Arbab Ghulam Rahim (born on September 15, 1957) remained the Chief Minister of the Sindh Government during the time period of 2004-2007.

Early life of Arbab Ghulam Rahim

Arbab Ghulam Rahim was born in the area of DÄ±plo Tehsil of Tharparkar District. Arbab Ghulam Rahim is believed to be the youngest of five siblings. He was born in a very conservative atmosphere and acquired his early education in his village. Then up to Seven grade he studied in the city of Mirpurkhas, from 8th class he joined the institute of Cadet College Petaro which is considered to be one of the few reputed institutions of the province at that time. After passing out as a House Captain from Cadet College Petaro he joined the institute of Liaquat University of Medical and Health Sciences at the city of Jamshoro to study medicine, but after that transferred to the Jinnah Sindh Medical University, Karachi. After passing out from the institution of Jinnah Medical College with a MBBS degree, Arbab contested in the Local government election and was appointed as Nazim in the Mirpur Khas District..

Political career

Arbab Ghulam Rahim achieved the elections as a Member of National Assembly (MNA) 4 times, and as Member of the Provincial Assembly (MPA) from his constituency in the area of Tharparkar District various times.
Basically belonging to the Pakistan Muslim League (Q). Arbab was appointed to the Sindh Provincial Assembly during the year of 2002 general elections from PS-60 constituency defeating Engineer Gianchand Meghwar, and was previously inducted as a provincial minister. Consequently, he was appointed as the Chief Minister of Sindh in the year of 2004.
Arbab Ghulam Rahim was hit in the head with a shoe by the Agha Javed Pathan a supporter of Benazir Bhutto in the year of 2008. Arbab rarely goes to the Provincial Assembly, because he blames the ruling party of attempting to disgrace him in the assembly.
After the PML-Q was defeated in the year of 2008 general elections Arbab Rahim left the Pakistan to get settled down in United Arab Emirates in self-imposed exile.
On the day of 27 December 2011, Arbab Ghulam Rahim was elected as the president of the Pakistan Muslim League (Like-Minded) party. Arbab Ghulam Rahim came back to Pakistan the day of on 13, May, 2013.
